Welcome to UW MMA

We are a student run club at the University of Washington teaching mixed martial arts. We teach a blend of grappling and striking. Classes are held in the IMA on Monday and Thursday's.

Areas Covered

  • Striking
  • Grappling
  • Kicking
  • Takedowns

Requirments

This club is open to all stutdents at UW, no previous experience or gear. We do recomend picking up these gear pieces linked below, but nothing is required.

  • Gloves
  • Shin Guards
  • Thai Pads
