Meet Our Instructors

Archer

Hey all! My name’s Archer and I’m the head coach for Husky MMA. I’m a sophomore chemistry undergrad and Seattle native. I have been training in mixed martial arts for about 15 years and come from a proud Bruce Lee lineage. I’m striving to create a great community for training all martial arts here at UW. See y’all on the mats!

Antonio


Hello everyone! My name is Antonio and I am one of the striking coaches for Husky MMA. I am a junior here at UW studying sociology. I’ve lived in Seattle and the surrounding areas for my entire life and have been training in a variety of striking martial arts off and on for the past 5 years. My goal is to help create a welcoming MMA community here at UW, and to help members reach their goals. I look forward to working with you all!

Kai

Heyo! My name is Kai, and I am a striking coach with an emphasis on application sparring for Husky MMA. I’m a sophomore in the mechanical engineering department and have lived in the Seattle area for over a decade. I teach to help people reach sparring proficiency and hope to create a community that can continually help each other. Happy training!
