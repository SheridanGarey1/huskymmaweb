--------------------
    Flexbox Notes
--------------------
1. Justify items vs Align items
    Justify is for horizantal alignment
    Align is for vertical alignment
Options for Justify items
    center
    flex-start
    flex-end
    space-around
    space-between

Options for Align items
    center
    flex-start   (top)
    flex-end      (bottom)
    stretch
    baseline
